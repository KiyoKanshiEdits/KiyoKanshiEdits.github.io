Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how KiyoKanshiEdits ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our application and TikTok integration. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

KiyoKanshiEdits does not collect personal information from viewers or the general public. Our application operates solely on behalf of a single account owner (@KiyoKanshiEdits) and does not process data from any other users.

In the course of connecting to TikTok's Content Posting API, we may process the following information relating to the account owner only:

  • TikTok account access tokens (used solely to authenticate and post content)
  • Basic profile information as provided by TikTok's API (username, account ID)

2. How We Use Information

Any information accessed through TikTok's API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate the @KiyoKanshiEdits TikTok account
  • Upload and publish video content to the @KiyoKanshiEdits TikTok account

We do not use this information for any other purpose, and we do not share it with any third parties.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored locally on the account owner's device and are not transmitted to any external servers operated by us. No personal data is stored in databases or cloud services controlled by KiyoKanshiEdits.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy, available at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy. We are not responsible for TikTok's data practices.

5. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us
